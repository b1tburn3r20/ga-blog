My Journey

My journey into the world of software development began in a place some might not expect: the gaming world. I was once an avid gamer, spending countless hours immersing myself in virtual worlds. However, there came a point when I started to feel like I was wasting my life away, and I yearned for something more fulfilling.

That's when I stumbled upon a game called Bitburner. Set in a cyberpunk world where major tech industries have taken over and created a massive wealth disparity, people are forced to become hackers to survive. This game was a turning point for me, as it introduced me to the basics of JavaScript.

Inspired by my experience with Bitburner, I ventured on my own and started learning Python. I was fascinated by the endless possibilities that programming offered, and my passion for coding grew stronger each day. In my pursuit of knowledge, I came across an online course by Mosh Hamedani, a well-respected figure in the industry. His course helped me master HTML and CSS, and further deepen my understanding of JavaScript.

Eager to expand my skillset, I continued to invest in my education by purchasing more courses on React JS, TypeScript, SQL, Angular, and Node.js. Each day, I dedicate time to learning and applying new concepts through various projects. This hands-on approach has allowed me to continually refine my skills and stay motivated on this journey.

Now, as I look back on my transition from a gamer to a software developer, I am proud of the progress I've made and grateful for the opportunities that have come my way. I'm excited to continue exploring the world of programming.
